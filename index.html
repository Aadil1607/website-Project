Paris

Hotels

Picture entering the elegance of The Ritz Paris, nestled at 15 Place Vendôme, where every detail exudes timeless charm. From its majestic architecture to its inviting lobby and beautifully decorated rooms, The Ritz offers a luxurious escape in the heart of Paris, ideal for creating lasting family memories. Delight in exquisite French and international cuisine at its renowned restaurants, including the iconic Hemingway Bar, making every meal a delightful family experience. With top-notch service and amenities like a world-class spa, The Ritz guarantees a memorable and indulgent stay for the whole family.

Entertainment

Paris is a family-friendly cultural paradise, brimming with entertainment options for all ages. Enjoy world-class theater at venues like Théâtre de la Ville, iconic concerts at Olympia Hall, and the legendary cabaret show at Moulin Rouge. Explore cinematic treasures at Le Grand Rex and marvel at masterpieces in renowned museums like the Louvre. Experience vibrant street performances, festivals, and family-friendly nightlife in districts like Pigalle. Paris, with its blend of art, music, and diverse cultural experiences, ensures there's something captivating for every family member

Competition


The 2024 Summer Olympics in Paris, scheduled from July 26 to August 11, promise a dazzling showcase of athleticism. Iconic venues across the city, including the Stade de France, will host diverse sports, from traditional to modern. The event emphasizes sustainability and inclusivity, embodying the spirit of unity. Against the backdrop of Parisian landmarks, these Olympics are poised to celebrate not only athletic excellence but also the cultural richness of the host city.
